# 📈 Reseller OS

A lightweight, offline-capable mobile CRM and profit calculator built for professional Vinted, Marktplaats, and vintage shop resellers. 

Designed for the "long game," this tool is mathematically structured to filter out low-margin flips and focus strictly on high-ROI assets, specifically targeting a minimum €15 profit margin per item.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Financial Calculator:** Automatically factors in asking price, shipping logistics, and platform fees (e.g., Vinted's Buyer Protection Fee) to show your true Net Return before you ever spend a cent.
* **Market Analyzer:** Built-in deep search buttons instantly query Vinted and Marktplaats. Input the market highs and lows to generate a visual "Sweet Spot" gauge for pricing your assets.
* **The Asset Vault:** A local-storage inventory CRM. Track deployed capital, projected yields, and realized profits. Mark items as "Liquidated" to keep a running ledger of your actual take-home cash. Includes CSV export for tax and accounting purposes.
* **Comms Suite:** Copy-and-paste negotiation templates (in Dutch and English) engineered to defend your profit margins against lowball offers.
* **100% Offline Capable:** Built as a static web application. Once loaded on your phone, the calculator and vault function completely offline—perfect for sourcing deep inside concrete thrift shops with zero cell reception.

## 📱 How to Install (iOS / iPhone)

Because this app utilizes local browser storage, it does not require an App Store download. 

1. Open Safari on your iPhone.
2. Navigate to your live GitHub Pages link (e.g., `https://yourusername.github.io/your-repo-name/`).
3. Tap the **Share icon** (the square with the arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add** in the top right corner. 

The Reseller OS will now live on your home screen and function as a standalone, full-screen mobile app.

## 🧠 The Workflow

1. **Source:** In a thrift shop or scrolling Vinted? Tap the Camera icon to snap a photo of the item. 
2. **Analyze:** Hit the deep search buttons to check live market rates. Input the market low/high into the Analyzer to find your target sell price.
3. **Calculate:** Input the buy price and shipping. The app will generate a financial receipt. If the net return clears the €15 margin, it gives you a green **Acquire** signal.
4. **Store:** Save the asset to your Vault. The total capital deployed and expected yield on your dashboard will update instantly. 
5. **Liquidate:** When the item sells, mark it liquidated in your Vault and input the final sale price to log your realized yield.
